FoodCast: Forecasting Food Demand to Reduce Waste

A data-driven solution to optimize food production in dining halls and restaurants, minimizing food waste, and saving resources.

About Us

Our Mission to Reduce Food Waste

Overestimation in food demand leads to significant food waste in dining halls and restaurants, causing unnecessary costs and environmental impact. FoodCast is designed to solve the problem of overestimated food demands that lead to waste. We are passionate about this problem because it’s something we’ve experienced firsthand. We hate seeing food go to waste when it doesn’t have to.

FoodCast optimizes pre-meal preparation to ensure that the right foods are produced in the right quantities, reducing overproduction, minimizing waste, and ensuring that the right foods are available. We’re tackling inefficiencies that affect us all.

Features

Main Features of FoodCast

Our solution integrates real-time data collection and advanced machine learning models to predict food demand with greater accuracy, ensuring efficient use of resources and minimizing food waste.

Predictive Analytics

Using advanced time-series models and real-time data, we forecast the food demand for the next day with high accuracy.

Data-Driven Decisions

Integrating various data points including weather, events, and historical consumption, we fine-tune demand predictions.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous data collection with weight sensors and object detection to track food consumption and adjust forecasts.

Waste Reduction

By predicting the right amount of food, we reduce waste and ensure efficient resource allocation.
